About

My name is Rachel Norman. I am a graphic design student at Algonquin College. I have created this website as a portfolio page to display some of my designs. I enjoy and have experience in graphic design, photography, videography and more. Most of the pieces have been created within the graphic design program.

I first started enjoying art after receiving an adult colouring book for my birthday. I then started to draw my own types of designs which then progressed to more realistic artwork with the help of art class in school. I then discovered the communications technology course in high school which introduced me to graphic design, animation, photography and videography.
